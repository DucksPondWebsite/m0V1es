Grown Ups

Grown Ups is a 2010 American comedy film directed by Dennis Dugan and written by Adam Sandler and Fred Wolf. The film stars Sandler, Kevin James, Chris Rock, David Spade, and Rob Schneider as five lifelong friends who reunite for a Fourth of July weekend after their childhood basketball coach dies.
