Halloween (2018)

Laurie Strode comes to her final confrontation with Michael Myers, the masked figure who has haunted her since she narrowly escaped his killing spree on Halloween night four decades ago. With her family caught in the crossfire, Laurie must find the strength to end Michael's reign of terror once and for all. Directed by David Gordon Green, this film brings a chilling new chapter to the iconic horror franchise, blending suspense, horror, and a riveting showdown between good and evil.
