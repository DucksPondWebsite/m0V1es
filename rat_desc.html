Ratatouille

Ratatouille is a 2007 American computer-animated comedy film produced by Pixar and released by Walt Disney Pictures. The film follows a rat named Remy who dreams of becoming a great chef and tries to achieve his goal by forming an unlikely alliance with a young kitchen worker at a famous restaurant.
