Free Guy

Free Guy is a 2021 American action comedy film directed by Shawn Levy and written by Matt Lieberman and Zak Penn. The film stars Ryan Reynolds as a bank teller who discovers that he is actually a non-player character (NPC) in an open-world video game and decides to become the hero of his own story, one that he can rewrite himself. With the help of a player, he tries to prevent the game's developers from shutting down his world.
